# Roofing Lead Page - Acceptance Checklist

**Before delivering to client, verify ALL items:**

---

## ✅ HONESTY CHECK

- [ ] Page is clearly marked as "SAMPLE DEMO" or customized for actual business
- [ ] No fake testimonials or reviews
- [ ] No fabricated years-in-business claims
- [ ] No invented metrics (leads generated, etc.)
- [ ] Contact info matches actual business
- [ ] Service area is accurate
- [ ] Services listed are actually offered

## ✅ CLARITY CHECK

- [ ] Business name is clear and prominent
- [ ] Services are easy to understand
- [ ] Contact methods are obvious
- [ ] CTA buttons are action-oriented
- [ ] Value proposition is clear
- [ ] No confusing jargon or tech terms
- [ ] Mobile users can understand immediately

## ✅ TRUST CHECK

- [ ] Licensed/insured mentioned if applicable
- [ ] Service area is clearly defined
- [ ] Contact info is complete and visible
- [ ] Form asks for reasonable information
- [ ] No pressure tactics or false urgency
- [ ] Professional design and layout
- [ ] Clear next steps for visitors

## ✅ CONVERSION CHECK

- [ ] Primary CTA above the fold
- [ ] Contact form is simple (5 fields max)
- [ ] Phone number is clickable on mobile
- [ ] Email is mailto: linked
- [ ] Multiple contact points available
- [ ] Form submission gives confirmation
- [ ] Clear value before asking for info

## ✅ MOBILE USABILITY CHECK

- [ ] Page loads fast on mobile (<3s)
- [ ] Text is readable without zooming
- [ ] Buttons are finger-friendly size
- [ ] Form fields are easy to tap
- [ ] Navigation works on touch
- [ ] No horizontal scrolling
- [ ] Images scale properly

## ✅ CUSTOMIZATION CHECK

- [ ] Business name replaced throughout
- [ ] City/state updated
- [ ] Phone number correct
- [ ] Email address correct
- [ ] Service area matches business
- [ ] Services list is accurate
- [ ] Brand colors applied (if requested)

## ✅ PRODUCTION READINESS

- [ ] Contact form sends to correct email
- [ ] Form has spam protection
- [ ] Page has basic meta tags
- [ ] Images are optimized
- [ ] No broken links
- [ ] SSL certificate active
- [ ] Page validates (no console errors)

## ✅ NO FAKE DATA

- [ ] No invented review counts
- [ ] No fake "years in business"
- [ ] No fabricated team size
- [ ] No made-up certifications
- [ ] No false partnerships
- [ ] No imaginary awards
- [ ] All claims are verifiable

---

## Delivery Package Contents

**Must include when delivering to client:**

1. **Live page URL** (on their domain or subdomain)
2. **Admin access** (if applicable)
3. **Source code** (if requested)
4. **Documentation**:
   - How to update contact info
   - How form submissions work
   - Basic maintenance notes
5. **Support contact** for 7-day post-delivery questions

---

## Client Handoff Script

"Here's your roofing lead page: [URL]

Key features:
- Mobile-friendly design
- Contact form sends leads to [their email]
- Service area shows [their cities]
- All your services listed

To update anything in the future:
1. Contact info: [instructions]
2. Services list: [instructions]
3. Service area: [instructions]

The page is now live and capturing leads. Test the form to make sure it reaches your email.

Any questions in the next 7 days, just reply here."

---

## Red Flags (Do NOT Deliver If)

❌ Client provided fake business info  
❌ Contact email doesn't work (bounces)  
❌ Services listed aren't actually offered  
❌ Client wants fake reviews added  
❌ Business appears inactive/closed  
❌ Legal requirements not met (licensing claims)
